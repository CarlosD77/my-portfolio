Need to impress someone?

We have the secret.

Posted by LifHacktory on August 28, 2022

The Secret

Whether you're interviewing for a job, on a first date, hanging out with your friends, or trying to meet new people, this handy trick will get anyone to like you! The best part is, anyone can do it! The secret: when you don't know what to say, start listing random facts off the top of your head! Pretty soon, people will be so impressed by your knowledge and intrigued by your facts that they'll always want to be around you!

Examples

It's best if you research your own facts, but here are a few examples to get you started.

  • Sonic's grasshopper mousse pie blizzard can't be flipped upside down due to whipped cream on top.
  • X-rays are incapable of detecting real diamonds.
  • German chocolate cake was first made in Texas.
